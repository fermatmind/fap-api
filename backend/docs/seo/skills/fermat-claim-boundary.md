# Fermat Claim Boundary

Status: draft internal operator skill.

This document adapts upstream marketing/copywriting/CRO thinking into FermatMind claim review boundaries. It is a review checklist only. It does not execute rewrites, mutate CMS, publish content, change frontend copy, enqueue Search Channel items, generate pSEO, or approve claim overrides.

## Core Rule

Use assessment language as decision support and self-understanding. Do not present FermatMind as a diagnosis product, treatment product, hiring-fit product, salary predictor, turnover predictor, career-success predictor, or complete personalized career recommender.

## MBTI Boundary

Allowed:

- personality type interpretation.
- communication/workstyle tendencies.
- bounded decision support.
- snapshot-based career exploration.
- report insights as reflective guidance.

Forbidden:

- MBTI decides income.
- MBTI predicts turnover.
- MBTI guarantees career outcomes.
- MBTI determines the best job.
- MBTI hiring suitability.

## Big Five Boundary

Allowed:

- trait-vector explanation.
- workstyle tendency.
- behavioral pattern framing.
- exploratory guidance.
- evidence-backed explanation where grounded.

Forbidden:

- Big Five precise career matching.
- Big Five predicts job performance.
- Big Five hiring fit.
- career success prediction.
- deterministic personality-to-job claims.

## RIASEC Boundary

Allowed:

- Holland career interest assessment.
- six-dimensional interest vector.
- interest signal.
- career direction reference.
- exploratory guidance.

Forbidden:

- RIASEC ranks your best career.
- RIASEC recommends a precise career.
- job fit guarantee.
- hiring suitability.
- career success or salary guarantee.

Required statement:

RIASEC is a real Holland interest assessment and six-dimensional interest vector, but current runtime is not a completed active RIASEC-to-career recommender.

## IQ Boundary

Allowed:

- reasoning pattern.
- modeled index.
- confidence-bound interpretation.
- for reference only.

Forbidden:

- clinical IQ diagnosis.
- definitive intelligence measurement.
- globally most accurate.
- official certification.
- treatment, disability, or clinical claims.

## EQ Boundary

Allowed:

- self-assessment.
- communication tendency.
- emotion-awareness signal.
- interpersonal reflection.
- for reference only.

Forbidden:

- clinical diagnosis.
- mental health determination.
- guaranteed relationship/work outcome.
- treatment or cure framing.

## Clinical / SDS / Anxiety / Depression Boundary

Allowed:

- self-assessment.
- screening.
- non-diagnostic.
- not a substitute for professional help.
- professional-help bounded guidance.
- crisis/help-seeking notice where appropriate.

Forbidden:

- diagnosis.
- clinical diagnosis.
- confirms you have a condition.
- treatment advice as medical advice.
- cure.
- emergency protocol claims beyond approved safety copy.

## Career Recommendation / Career Guides Boundary

Allowed:

- career direction reference.
- workstyle tendency.
- interest signal.
- exploratory guidance.
- decision support.
- occupation information from backend-authoritative Career Graph.

Forbidden:

- precise career recommendation.
- best career for you.
- perfect job match.
- hiring fit.
- job suitability guarantee.
- career success prediction.
- salary guarantee.

Required statement:

Career Graph is real backend authority, but psychometric semantic recommendation is partial.

## Research / Salary / Turnover Boundary

Allowed:

- aggregate-level trend.
- modeled index.
- directional signal.
- methodology-bound interpretation.
- sample and limitation disclosures.

Forbidden:

- individual salary prediction.
- personal turnover prediction.
- MBTI determines income.
- MBTI predicts turnover.
- causal claims beyond the study design.
- guaranteed business outcome.

## Digital PR Boundary

Allowed:

- methodology-forward summaries.
- aggregate findings.
- cautious outreach framing.
- clear limitation and source notes.

Forbidden:

- sensationalized clinical/career claims.
- unsupported "global first/best/most accurate" claims.
- paid backlink schemes.
- fake academic identity.
- directory submissions with duplicate or overclaimed descriptions.

## Result / Report / Paywall Copy Boundary

Allowed:

- explain what the free result includes.
- explain what the paid report adds.
- use clear unlock CTA.
- describe report depth without guaranteeing outcomes.
- preserve non-diagnostic and exploratory language.

Forbidden:

- fear-based medical pressure.
- "unlock to know your diagnosis".
- "pay to find your best career".
- guaranteed salary/career/hiring outcome.
- false urgency or unsupported authority.

## Review Checklist

For any claim-sensitive copy:

1. Identify product family and page surface.
2. Identify whether copy is public, draft, report, paywall, email, PDF, share, Research, or Digital PR.
3. Check forbidden phrases and implied claims.
4. Check whether safer phrasing still implies a guarantee in context.
5. Confirm visible evidence and methodology where a research claim is made.
6. Confirm backend/CMS authority source.
7. Confirm no frontend fallback becomes source-of-truth.
8. If unsafe, mark blocked or needs human review. Do not auto-rewrite.
