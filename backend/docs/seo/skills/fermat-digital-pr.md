# Fermat Digital PR

Status: draft internal operator skill.

This document adapts upstream `directory-submissions`, `competitors`, `customer-research`, and `emails` thinking into FermatMind's human-approved Digital PR workflow. It is not authorization to send email, submit directories, mutate CMS, publish content, enqueue Search Channel items, buy links, scrape contacts, or deploy.

## Authority Rules

- Digital PR signals are not URL Truth.
- Backlinks, referrals, mentions, and directory listings are observation signals.
- Backend/CMS/URL Truth remains authority.
- Search Channel has no side effects from Digital PR work.
- Every send or follow-up requires exact user approval.
- Claim linter review is required before pitch approval.

## Digital PR Wave 2 Governance

Digital PR Wave 2 should focus on claim-safe Research Hub and methodology assets. It must be human-paced, tracked, and evidence-bound.

Allowed:

- target list review.
- pitch draft review.
- claim lint review.
- one manually approved send.
- one manually approved follow-up.
- mention/backlink/referral observation.

Not allowed:

- bulk outreach.
- paid backlinks.
- transactional backlink asks.
- scraping/private email harvesting.
- mass directory submissions.
- AI-generated email send without human approval.
- Search Channel enqueue or submit.
- CMS mutation.

## HRZone Follow-up Handling

For HRZone or similar targets:

- verify prior send status.
- apply one-follow-up limit.
- review exact follow-up copy.
- run claim boundary review.
- require exact user approval before sending.
- record follow-up date and outcome.

If the follow-up window has passed, mark as observe/defer rather than sending automatically.

## HREC First-send Handling

For HREC or similar first-send targets:

- confirm target belongs to whitelist.
- confirm pitch aligns with target audience.
- confirm Research asset is public, claim-safe, and backend-authoritative.
- confirm exact email copy.
- require exact user approval before sending.

## Research Hub Pitch Boundary

Pitch language must remain:

- methodology-forward.
- aggregate-level.
- cautious.
- evidence-bound.
- transparent about sample/limitations.

No pitch may claim:

- MBTI predicts salary or turnover.
- Big Five predicts job performance.
- RIASEC ranks a best career.
- FermatMind diagnoses clinical conditions.
- FermatMind guarantees career, hiring, salary, or treatment outcomes.

## Target List Review

Targets should be reviewed for:

- relevance.
- editorial fit.
- audience fit.
- likely authority value.
- claim-risk sensitivity.
- contact legitimacy.
- no private harvesting.
- no pay-for-link policy.

Do not force FermatMind into unrelated directories or publications.

## Manual Send Rule

Every send/follow-up requires:

- exact approved target.
- exact approved sender.
- exact approved copy.
- exact approval phrase from the user.
- tracking record prepared before send.

No AI-generated email send without human approval.

## Tracking Fields

Every outreach target must include:

- `target_name`.
- `target_category`.
- `contact_channel`.
- `human_sender`.
- `sent_at`.
- `status`.
- `follow_up_due_at`.
- `follow_up_sent_at`.
- `mention_url`.
- `mention_type`.
- `backlink_url`.
- `referral_source`.
- `referral_sessions`.
- `branded_query_lift_proxy`.
- `claim_lint_state`.
- `notes`.

## Mention / Referral / Backlink Tracking

Track:

- mention URL and type.
- backlink URL and attributes where visible.
- referral source.
- referral sessions.
- assisted conversions where privacy-safe.
- branded query lift proxy.

Do not treat these signals as page truth, revenue truth, or indexing truth. They are observation signals only.

## Brand Lift Proxy

Permitted proxy observations:

- branded query trend.
- direct/referral landing sessions.
- mention volume.
- known publication live links.
- Research asset referral paths.

Do not over-attribute revenue or SEO ranking changes to a single PR mention without supporting data.

## Directory Submission Boundary

No mass directory submissions.

Directory work is allowed only as:

- readiness review.
- target fit review.
- claim-safe listing copy review.
- manual submission after exact approval.
- tracking setup.

No paid links, no duplicate boilerplate spam, no fake categories, no Search Channel side effects.

## Readiness Checklist

- claim-safe pitch.
- target whitelist.
- tracking fields complete.
- one-follow-up window defined.
- no paid link language.
- no bulk send.
- approved sender.
- approved exact email copy.
- no Search Channel action.
- Research asset is backend/CMS-authoritative and public.
- no clinical/career/salary/turnover overclaim.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if the request requires:

- bulk send.
- private email scraping.
- paid backlink negotiation.
- CMS mutation.
- Search Channel action.
- URL submission.
- unreviewed AI-generated send.
- unsupported clinical/career/salary/turnover claim.
